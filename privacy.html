Lock School — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Lock School is A field locksmith's trainer. Learn commercial door hardware by model — parts, functions, and terminology, with real manufacturer diagrams. This policy explains what we collect, why, how long we keep it, and your choices. It applies to the Lock School mobile app and website. Questions or requests: justen.phillips88@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Account details — your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Activity logs — sign-ins, sign-ups, and visits. Each log includes your IP address and, derived from it, an approximate location (city / country), your network provider (ISP), and connection type (e.g. mobile, broadband, VPN / datacenter). Visits are recorded for guests too, before you create an account.
  • Learning activity — for signed-in users, your progress (lessons completed, XP, quiz results) and any optional lesson feedback you submit, so your progress syncs across devices.

We do not collect contacts, photos, location from device GPS, or advertising identifiers.

Why we collect it

To run, secure, and improve Lock School — operating your account, syncing your progress, understanding usage, and detecting abuse or fraudulent access. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Payments

Optional PRO subscriptions are processed on the web by Stripe; we never see or store your card details. The Android app does not sell anything in-app.

How long we keep it

Activity logs (visits and auth events, including IP and location data) are automatically deleted after 90 days. Account details and learning progress are kept while your account exists, and removed when you delete your account.

Who we share it with

We share data only with the service providers that run Lock School, under their own terms: Supabase (hosting, authentication, database) and Stripe (payments, web only). We do not sell data or share it with advertisers.

Your choices and rights

You can request a copy of your data, or ask us to delete your account and associated logs, at any time by emailing justen.phillips88@gmail.com. Password resets are handled through a time-limited emailed link. You can use Lock School's free lessons as a guest without an account.

Children

Lock School is intended for users aged 13 and over and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
